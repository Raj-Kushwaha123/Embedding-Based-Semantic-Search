ABOUT THE COMPANY
-----------------
NovaTech Solutions is a technology company headquartered in Bangalore, India,
with satellite offices in Hyderabad, Pune, and Guwahati. Founded in 2018, we
specialize in enterprise AI solutions, cloud-native software development, and
data analytics platforms. Our mission is to democratize cutting-edge technology
for businesses of every size. As of January 2026, NovaTech employs over 850
professionals across engineering, product, design, sales, and operations.

Our core values:
  1. Innovation First — we encourage experimentation and creative problem-solving.
  2. Customer Obsession — every product decision is driven by customer impact.
  3. Transparency — open communication at every level of the organization.
  4. Continuous Learning — we invest in our people through upskilling programs.


LEAVE POLICY
------------
All full-time employees are entitled to the following paid leave per calendar
year (January 1 – December 31). Leave balances do not carry over unless
explicitly approved by the HR department.

  • Sick Leave: 12 days per year. A medical certificate is required for absences
    exceeding 3 consecutive working days. Unused sick leave is not encashable.
  • Casual Leave: 15 days per year. Casual leave cannot be taken for more than
    3 consecutive days without prior manager approval.
  • Earned / Privilege Leave: 18 days per year, accrued at 1.5 days per month.
    Earned leave can be carried forward up to a maximum of 30 days. Excess leave
    will be encashed at the end of the fiscal year at the basic salary rate.
  • Maternity Leave: 26 weeks of paid leave for female employees, in accordance
    with the Maternity Benefit Act, 2017.
  • Paternity Leave: 10 working days of paid leave, to be availed within 3
    months of the child's birth or adoption.
  • Bereavement Leave: 5 days of paid leave in the event of the death of an
    immediate family member (spouse, parent, child, or sibling).
  • Public Holidays: 12 gazetted holidays per year as announced by the company.


WORK FROM HOME POLICY
---------------------
NovaTech follows a hybrid work model. Employees may work remotely up to 3 days
per week, subject to team-level agreements and manager approval. The following
guidelines apply:

  • Employees must be available during core hours: 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM IST.
  • A stable internet connection (minimum 20 Mbps) is required.
  • All remote work must be performed from within India unless international
    remote work is pre-approved by HR and Legal.
  • Employees working from home must use the company-provided VPN for accessing
    internal systems and must adhere to IT security policies at all times.
  • Team leads may designate mandatory in-office days for collaboration, sprint
    planning, or client meetings.
  • Employees who prefer fully remote arrangements may apply through the HR
    portal; these are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.


PERFORMANCE REVIEW PROCESS
--------------------------
NovaTech conducts performance reviews on a biannual cycle (April and October).

  1. Self-Assessment: Employees complete a self-assessment form covering goals
     achieved, challenges faced, and areas of growth.
  2. Peer Feedback: At least two peer reviews are collected through the internal
     360-degree feedback tool.
  3. Manager Review: The reporting manager evaluates the employee on a 5-point
     scale across key competencies: Technical Excellence, Collaboration,
     Ownership, Communication, and Innovation.
  4. Calibration: Department heads participate in a calibration meeting to
     ensure consistency and fairness across teams.
  5. Feedback Meeting: The manager conducts a one-on-one feedback session to
     discuss ratings, growth areas, and career development plans.

Performance ratings:
  5 — Exceptional: Consistently exceeds expectations; strong leadership impact.
  4 — Exceeds Expectations: Frequently delivers above the expected level.
  3 — Meets Expectations: Reliably meets role requirements.
  2 — Needs Improvement: Performance gaps identified; improvement plan created.
  1 — Unsatisfactory: Significant underperformance; formal PIP initiated.

Annual increments and bonuses are directly tied to performance ratings.


EMPLOYEE BENEFITS
-----------------

    up to two dependent children. Sum insured: ₹5,00,000 per year. Dental and
    vision coverage is included in the base plan.
  • Gym & Wellness: Monthly reimbursement of up to ₹2,500 for gym memberships,

    over a 4-year period with a 1-year cliff.

    matched by the company. Gratuity as per the Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972.


CODE OF CONDUCT
---------------
All employees are expected to uphold the highest standards of professional
behavior:

  • Treat every colleague, client, and partner with respect and dignity.
  • Harassment, discrimination, or bullying of any form will result in
    disciplinary action up to and including termination.
  • Conflicts of interest must be disclosed to the Ethics Committee promptly.
  • Company property, including laptops, software licenses, and data, must be
    used solely for authorized business purposes.
  • Employees must not engage in any activity that could damage the company's
    reputation or violate applicable laws and regulations.
  • Gifts or hospitality from vendors exceeding ₹5,000 in value must be
    reported to the Compliance team.


IT SECURITY GUIDELINES
----------------------
Protecting company and customer data is everyone's responsibility.

  • Passwords must be at least 12 characters long, include uppercase, lowercase,
    numbers, and special characters. Passwords must be changed every 90 days.
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all internal systems
    including email, VPN, GitHub, and cloud consoles.
  • Never share your credentials with anyone, including IT support staff.
  • All company laptops must have full-disk encryption enabled and the approved
    endpoint protection software installed.
  • Sensitive data (PII, financial records, source code) must not be stored on
    personal devices or shared via unapproved channels (e.g., personal email,
    WhatsApp, or public cloud storage).
  • Report any suspected security incident or phishing attempt immediately to
    security@novatech.com or via the #security-incidents Slack channel.
  • USB drives and external storage devices are disabled on company machines by
    default. Exceptions require written approval from the CISO.
